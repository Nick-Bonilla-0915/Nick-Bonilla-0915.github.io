(Monster) Hunting Genre Experience

Description

This was an essay I wrote for my English Composition I class. The topic of this essay was genres, but not in the sense most people would think of.
In this context, a genre is a type of writing for a certain purpose, like a public speech or a manual for a car. I had originally planned on writing
about public speeches as my genre, but my professor told me that was too broad of a subject. I decided to opt for a much smaller genre, which was the
skill descriptions in the Monster Hunter video game series. I felt I was much more versed in this topic, as Monster Hunter is a series I greatly enjoy.

Skills Learned

  • Learned how to connect abstract topics for the purpose of writing.
  • Experienced writing at a college level.
  • Gained experience in finding and using resources in my work.
  • Effectively used feedback from peers to improve essay.
